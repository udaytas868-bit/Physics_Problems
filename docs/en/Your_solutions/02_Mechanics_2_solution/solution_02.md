## Problem 2: Range Optimization
The range formula is $R(\theta) = \frac{v_0^2 \sin(2\theta)}{g}$.
To find the maximum range, we take the derivative with respect to $\theta$ and set it to zero:
$\frac{dR}{d\theta} = \frac{v_0^2}{g} \cdot \cos(2\theta) \cdot 2 = 0$
$\cos(2\theta) = 0 \implies 2\theta = 90^\circ \implies \theta = 45^\circ$
Thus, the maximum range is achieved at $45^\circ$.
