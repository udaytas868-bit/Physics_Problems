# Problem 8: Circular Motion

## Formula
Centripetal acceleration: $a_c = \omega^2 R = \left(\frac{2\pi}{T}\right)^2 R$

---

## Solution
Earth's Radius $R \approx 6,378,000 \text{ m}$
Period $T = 24 \text{ h} = 86,400 \text{ s}$
$$a_c = \frac{4\pi^2}{(86400)^2} \cdot 6,378,000 \approx 0.0337 \text{ m/s}^2$$
