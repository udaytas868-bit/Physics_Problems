# Problem 9: Momentum Comparison

## Formula
Momentum $p = mv$ (mass must be in kg)

---

## Solution
1. **Fly:** $m = 2\text{g} = 0.002\text{kg}, \quad v = 10\text{m/s}$
$p_{fly} = 0.002 \cdot 10 = 0.02 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$

2. **Tennis Ball:** $m = 60\text{g} = 0.06\text{kg}, \quad v = 1\text{m/s}$
$p_{ball} = 0.06 \cdot 1 = 0.06 \text{ kg}\cdot\text{m/s}$

**Result:** The tennis ball has a greater momentum.
